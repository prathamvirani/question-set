Strengthening National Integration

An interactive exploration of the continuous process of building unity within Malaysia's diverse society.

Unity: The Desired Goal

Unity is the ultimate aspiration, a process that unites society through a shared identity and common values.

✅ Moments of Unity

These are everyday situations where Malaysians come together, showcasing a natural sense of togetherness.

  • Supporting national sports teams.
  • Helping one another during natural disasters.
  • Celebrating diverse festivals and weddings.

⚠️ Moments of Difference

These are instances where sensitivities are not respected, leading to friction and division.

  • Instances of racism and discrimination.
  • Disputes over language, culture, and religion.
  • Friction from disrespecting cultural sensitivities.

Cohesion: The Level Achieved

Cohesion is the state of harmony built on tolerance and respect. Explore its key components below.

National Cohesion

The unification of various groups into a single entity, guided by the **Federal Constitution** and the **Rukun Negara**. It reflects our society's dynamic evolution into a modern, industrial, and cosmopolitan nation.

Social Cohesion vs. Social Deficit

Social Cohesion: A state of harmony built on positive social norms like politeness and respect. It's achieved by overcoming inequality and mistrust.

Social Deficit: The gaps in perception and needs among different groups. Key areas include ethnicity, religion, class, and the urban/rural divide.

The National Ethos

The set of shared characteristics that binds Malaysians together, built on four pillars: Shared Values, Shared Feelings, Shared Beliefs, and a Shared Identity.

Unification: The Process of Effort

Unification is the continuous effort to bridge social deficits by creating "integration sites" at all levels of society.

Thinning Ethnic Boundaries

The goal is to make the lines drawn by cultural and religious differences thinner to encourage more interaction and understanding, moving away from isolation.

Fostering Cross-Ethnic Relations

Promoting collective activities and relationships across different groups is vital for strengthening social cohesion and building a truly integrated society.

Core Principles of Unification

🔄
Bargaining

A process of give-and-take to reach mutually beneficial agreements.

🗣️
Negotiation

Open and respectful discussion to find common solutions to shared problems.

🕊️
Mediation

Using neutral parties to help resolve disputes and build bridges between communities.
