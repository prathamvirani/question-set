Bahasa Melayu: Friends & Family

Topic 2.3: A guide to talking about your friends and family.

1. Kawan (Friends)

In Bahasa Melayu, a friend can be referred to as kawan, rakan, or sahabat. These words can describe anyone from a close friend to a classmate or colleague.

Describing a Friend (Menceritakan tentang Kawan)

  • Nama kawan saya Siti Nurhaliza.
    My friend’s name is Siti Nurhaliza.
  • Dia dari Thailand.
    She is from Thailand.
  • Umurnya 30 tahun.
    She is 30 years old.
  • Hobi Siti Nurhaliza menyanyi.
    Siti Nurhaliza’s hobby is singing.
  • Dia ada sebuah kereta Proton Saga.
    She has a Proton Saga car.

2. Tatabahasa (Grammar)

Kata Tanya (Question Words)

These are essential words for asking for information about someone.

Bahasa Melayu English Example
SiapakahWhoSiapakah nama awak?
Di manakahWhereDi manakah rumah awak?
BilakahWhenBilakah awak akan datang?
BerapakahHow many/muchBerapakah umur awak?

Kata Ganti Nama Tunjuk (Demonstrative Pronouns)

These pronouns are used to point to specific people or objects.

Ini (This)

Used for things that are near.

Example: Ini ialah buku saya. (This is my book.)

Itu (That)

Used for things that are far.

Example: Itu ialah keretanya. (That is his/her car.)
