3. Tatabahasa: Kata Bantu (Grammar: Modals)

"Kata bantu" are function words, similar to modals in English, that add context like desire, ability, or obligation to a verb.

Mahu / Hendak - Want

Saya mahu pergi ke kelas.

I want to go to class.

Boleh - Can

Zhang Li boleh menyanyi.

Zhang Li can sing.

Mesti - Must

Awak mesti membayar sewa rumah.

You must pay the house rent.

Sedang - In the process of / -ing

Ibu sedang memasak.

Mother is cooking.
