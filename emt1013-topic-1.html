Engineering Mathematics: Algebra Test

Questions & Detailed Answers

Questions

1. Algebraic Properties

Explain the difference between the associative and distributive properties of algebra, providing a unique numerical example for each that demonstrates why subtraction is not associative and why division does not distribute from the left.

2. Order of Operations

Simplify the following expression by strictly adhering to the BODMAS/PEMDAS rule: $12 + (8^2 - 4 \times (5+2)) \div 6 - \sqrt{9}$

3. Proportionality

The electrical resistance `R` of a wire is directly proportional to its length `L` and inversely proportional to the square of its diameter `d`. If a wire 50 meters long with a diameter of 2 mm has a resistance of 0.5 ohms, what is the resistance of a wire made of the same material that is 30 meters long with a diameter of 1.5 mm?

4. Nested Brackets

Simplify the expression by first removing the innermost brackets and combining like terms: $3x^2 - [2y - \{4x(x - 3y) - (x^2 - 8xy)\}]$

5. Indices Simplification

Given the expression $(6a^3b^{-2}c)^2 \times (a^{-1}b^3c^{-2})^{1/2} \div [9(a^4b^2c^2)^{-1/2}]$, simplify it completely, leaving your answer with positive indices only.

6. Evaluating Powers

Without using a calculator, evaluate: $(\frac{81}{16})^{-3/4} \times (\frac{25}{9})^{-1/2}$

7. Exponential Equations

Solve for `x` in the equation: $4^{2x-1} = 5^{x+2}$. Give your answer correct to 4 significant figures.

8. Logarithm Properties

Given that $log_a 2 = p$ and $log_a 3 = q$, express $log_a( \frac{9\sqrt{a}}{4})$ in terms of `p` and `q`.

9. Logarithmic Equations

Solve the logarithmic equation for `y` in terms of `x`: $3 \log_b(y) - \log_b(x+1) = 2 \log_b(y) + \log_b(x^2)$

10. Radioactive Decay

The radioactive decay of an isotope is given by the formula $N(t) = N_0 e^{-\lambda t}$. If the substance has a half-life of 120 years, what percentage of the original substance will remain after 200 years?

11. Equations with 'e'

Find the value of `x` in the equation: $e^{2x} - 5e^x + 6 = 0$.

12. Polynomial Multiplication

Perform the polynomial multiplication and simplify the result: $(3x^2 - 2x + 5)(2x^2 + x - 4)$

13. Polynomial Long Division

Use long division to find the quotient and remainder when $(6x^4 - 5x^3 + 8x^2 - 7x + 1)$ is divided by $(2x - 1)$.

14. Factor Theorem

If $(x-2)$ is a factor of the polynomial $P(x) = x^3 + kx^2 - 3x - 6$, find the value of `k` and then find the other factors of the polynomial.

15. Simplifying Algebraic Fractions

Simplify the following algebraic fraction: $\frac{x+2}{x^2-4} - \frac{x-1}{x^2+x-2}$

16. Partial Fractions

Resolve the following expression into its partial fractions: $\frac{5x-4}{x^2 - x - 2}$

17. Partial Fractions (Repeated Factor)

Resolve the following expression with a repeated linear factor into partial fractions: $\frac{x^2 + 9x + 8}{(x-3)(x+2)^2}$

18. Partial Fractions (Quadratic Factor)

Resolve the expression with an irreducible quadratic factor into partial fractions: $\frac{3x^2 - 4x + 5}{(x-1)(x^2+2)}$

19. Combining Fractions

Combine the fractions and simplify: $\frac{3}{a-2} + \frac{2}{a+2} - \frac{4a}{a^2-4}$

20. Factorization by Grouping

Factorize the expression completely by grouping: $6ax - 15ay - 8bx + 20by$

21. Difference of Two Squares

Factorize the "difference of two squares": $81x^4 - 16y^8$.

22. Difference of Two Cubes

Factorize the cubic expression: $x^3 - 27y^3$.

23. Ideal Gas Law Application

The pressure `P`, volume `V`, and temperature `T` of a gas are related by the formula $PV = nRT$. Make `T` the subject of the formula. If the pressure is increased by 10% and the volume is decreased by 15%, what is the percentage change in the temperature?

24. Impedance Formula Rearrangement

The total impedance `Z` in an AC circuit is given by $Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2}$. Rearrange the formula to make $X_L$ the subject.
